Our Educational Approach

At GAPSAfrica, education is not only about information — it is about development, expression, and human potential.

The Reggio Emilia Approach

In 2026, we adopted the Reggio Emilia approach to guide how children learn, grow, and engage with the world around them.

What It Is

  • Child-centered learning
  • Exploration and creativity
  • Collaboration and expression

Why We Chose It

In underserved communities, education often limits creativity. This approach restores voice, confidence, and identity.

Why It Works

Learning becomes active, meaningful, and rooted in real life — not memorization.

Potential Impact

Confident children, stronger communities, and long-term sustainable change.
