EmpowerHer Initiative

Women in our community carry strength, knowledge, and resilience. EmpowerHer builds on that foundation by expanding opportunity, creativity, and economic independence.

The Foundation

Who They Are

Women in Nakivale are builders, caregivers, and leaders within their families and communities.

They manage households, support one another, and adapt daily with resilience and creativity.

EmpowerHer creates space for that strength to grow, expand, and generate sustainable income.

Building on Strength

This initiative provides tools, resources, and collaborative learning environments that allow women to extend what they already know into new forms of opportunity.

It is not about starting from zero — it is about unlocking and amplifying what is already present.

Expanding Skills into Opportunity

🧼 Soap & Ointment Making

🌱 Permaculture
